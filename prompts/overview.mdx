The Awesome Gemini Prompts collection is a curated set of prompt examples for use with Google's Gemini AI model. Prompts are organized by use case, ranging from everyday productivity tasks to creative writing, research, and advanced AI configurations.

<Tip>
  To use any prompt, copy it directly into Gemini and replace any bracketed placeholders (e.g., `[your topic]`) with your specific content. The more context you provide, the more tailored the response.
</Tip>

<Note>
  This collection is bilingual. Many prompts are available in both English and Chinese (Simplified). To improve Chinese-language responses, add the following line to any prompt: `使用简体中文回答，符合简体中文的表达习惯`
</Note>
