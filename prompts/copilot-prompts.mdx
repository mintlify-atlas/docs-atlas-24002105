---
title: "Copilot Prompts"
description: "Ready-to-use prompts for everyday tasks with Gemini — study plans, travel, writing, coding, and more"
---

Gemini works well as a daily productivity copilot. The prompts below cover a range of common tasks: planning, writing, analysis, coding, and creative work. Each prompt uses bracketed placeholders you can replace with your own details.

<Tip>
  Wherever you see `[placeholder]` text in a prompt, replace it with your specific information before sending. The more detail you provide, the better the output.
</Tip>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Productivity">
    ### Create a study plan

    ```text Create a study plan
    Create a 12-week study plan for learning a new language: Italian
    ```

    ### Find travel plans and packing lists

    ```text Travel plans
    Find hotels in Phuket for a week in March and suggest a packing list
    ```

    ### Summarize and analyze documents

    ```text Advanced summarization and analysis
    In Google Docs, please summarize this market research report into a few bullet points. Then, analyze the data to highlight the top three opportunities for my business, along with any potential risks I should be aware of.
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Creative">
    ### Write a blog post

    ```text Article writing
    write a medium-style blog post about [xx]
    ```

    <Tip>
      Replace `[xx]` with your topic. You can also specify tone, length, or target audience for more tailored output — for example: `write a medium-style blog post about remote work productivity, aimed at software engineers, around 800 words`.
    </Tip>

    ### Draft an introduction email

    ```text Email drafting
    Draft a short, friendly email introducing myself as a freelance [your expertise]. Include these points: [list your key skills]. End with a question to encourage a response.
    ```

    ### Brainstorm story ideas

    ```text Brainstorming and stories
    Give me three fantasy short story ideas that include a magical object and a reluctant hero.
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Technical">
    ### Debug code errors

    ```text Codes and problem-solving
    I'm getting the following error in my Android Studio project: [insert error message]. Here's the relevant code section: [insert code]. Can you help me debug this issue and suggest a fix?
    ```

    <Tip>
      Replace `[insert error message]` with the exact error text from your IDE, and `[insert code]` with the relevant code block. Including the full stack trace significantly improves diagnostic accuracy.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
