## What is Awesome Gemini Prompts?

Awesome Gemini Prompts is a community-maintained repository of prompt examples designed for use with [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/app) — the AI assistant built by Google that is capable of generating human-like text.

The project is organized by [LangGPT](https://github.com/langgptai), a community dedicated to structured prompt engineering for large language models. With **428 GitHub stars** and a **CC0-1.0 license**, the collection is freely available for anyone to use, adapt, and contribute to.

By providing Gemini with a well-crafted prompt, you can generate responses that continue a conversation, expand on a topic, solve problems, write content, or teach you something new. This repository gives you a starting point — and a community to grow with.

<Note>
  The LangGPT community maintains this collection and more prompt engineering resources at [https://github.com/langgptai](https://github.com/langgptai). Contributions are welcome — you can add your own prompts directly via GitHub.
</Note>

## What's included

The collection covers a wide range of use cases across both English and Chinese:

- **Copilot prompts** — practical everyday tasks like writing study plans, drafting emails, brainstorming, and summarizing documents
- **Thinking chain prompts** — advanced chain-of-thought frameworks for deep reasoning and self-critique
- **Learning methodology prompts** — structured tutors based on the PDCA-ASK methodology
- **System prompts** — official and community-sourced system prompts for Gemini 2.5 and the Gemini Pro (Bard) API
- **Specialized role prompts** — persona prompts for content creators, researchers, and more
- **Jailbreak prompts** — documented examples for research and awareness purposes

## Bilingual support

The repository is bilingual — content and prompts appear in both **English and Chinese (Simplified)**. Chinese users will find dedicated tips for getting better Simplified Chinese output from Gemini, including a recommended prompt phrase that avoids garbled output.

For more on Chinese language usage, see the [Quickstart](/quickstart) guide.

## Who it's for

This collection is useful for:

- **Developers and researchers** exploring Gemini's capabilities through prompt engineering
- **Content creators** looking for ready-to-use prompts for writing, social media, and ideation
- **Students and educators** using AI-assisted learning and structured thinking frameworks
- **Chinese-language users** seeking prompts optimized for Simplified Chinese output
- **Anyone curious** about what Gemini can do with the right prompt
