  <Accordion title="What is Google Gemini?">
    Google Gemini is Google's family of multimodal AI models, capable of understanding and generating text, code, images, audio, and video. It was previously known as **Bard** before being rebranded to Gemini in February 2024.

    Gemini is available through:
    - [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/app) — web interface
    - Google Gemini mobile app (iOS and Android)
    - Google Workspace (Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides)
    - [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com) — for developers
    - [Gemini API](https://ai.google.dev) — for programmatic access
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Gemini and Bard?">
    **Bard** was Google's original AI chatbot, launched in March 2023. It was rebranded to **Gemini** in February 2024, alongside the launch of the Gemini model family (Nano, Pro, Ultra). The underlying technology improved significantly with the rebrand.

    Gemini 2.5 Pro and Gemini 2.5 Flash are the current flagship models as of 2025.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I access Gemini?">
    **Free access:**
    - Visit [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/app) and sign in with a Google account
    - Use the Gemini mobile app

    **Gemini Advanced (paid):**
    - Subscribe to Google One AI Premium for access to Gemini 2.5 Pro and Gems

    **Developer access:**
    - Sign up at [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com) for free API access
    - Use the [Gemini API](https://ai.google.dev) with Python, JavaScript, or REST
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get better Chinese language responses?">
    Add this phrase to your prompt or system prompt to get consistent Simplified Chinese responses:

    ```
    使用简体中文回答，符合简体中文的表达习惯
    ```

    This tells Gemini to respond in Simplified Chinese following natural Chinese expression conventions. Place it at the beginning of your system prompt for best results.

    See the full guide at [Chinese Language Support](/guides/chinese-language).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the PDCA-ASK methodology?">
    PDCA-ASK is a learning framework that combines the classic PDCA quality cycle with active learning skills:

    - **P (Plan) — 问 (Ask)**: Generate a core question to anchor learning
    - **D (Do) — 联×用 (Connect × Apply)**: Connect to prior knowledge, apply in practice
    - **C (Check) — 思 (Think)**: Reflect deeply on results and gaps
    - **A (Act) — 问-迭代 (Ask-Iterate)**: Form new, better questions

    The [PDCA-ASK Learning Tutor](/guides/pdca-ask-learning) prompt implements this as an AI persona called 叮当猫 · 清静书斋.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What Gemini models are these prompts designed for?">
    Most prompts in this collection work best with:
    - **Gemini 2.5 Pro** — highest capability, best for complex reasoning tasks
    - **Gemini 2.5 Flash** — fast and efficient, suitable for most everyday prompts

    The Xiaohongshu persona prompt explicitly targets **Gemini 3.0 Pro**. The Google Antigravity prompts reference **Gemini 2.5 Flash** in the auto-researcher tool.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I use a system prompt with Gemini?">
    Via the Gemini API:

    ```python
    import google.generativeai as genai

    genai.configure(api_key="YOUR_API_KEY")
    model = genai.GenerativeModel(
        model_name="gemini-2.5-pro",
        system_instruction="Your system prompt here"
    )
    response = model.generate_content("Your question")
    ```

    Via Google AI Studio: Open a new chat → click the settings icon → paste your system instruction in the "System Instructions" field.

    See the full guide at [Gemini System Prompts](/guides/gemini-system-prompt).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are the jailbreak prompts safe to use?">
    The jailbreak prompts are documented for **research and educational purposes only**. Using them may:
    - Violate Google's [Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms)
    - Result in account suspension
    - Produce harmful content

    If you need adjusted content filtering for legitimate purposes (e.g., research, creative writing), use the official [Gemini API safety settings](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/safety-settings) instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Xiaohongshu (小红书)?">
    **Xiaohongshu** (小红书, literally "Little Red Book"), also known as **RED** in English, is a major Chinese social media and e-commerce platform. It combines Instagram-style visual content with Pinterest-style discovery and strong in-app shopping features.

    It has over 300 million monthly active users, primarily in China. The platform is particularly popular for lifestyle, beauty, fashion, travel, and food content.

    The [Xiaohongshu Creator](/prompts/xiaohongshu-creator) prompt in this collection is a monetization strategy expert for creators on this platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Google Antigravity?">
    **Antigravity** is described in the community-sourced prompts as "a powerful agentic AI coding assistant designed by the Google DeepMind team working on Advanced Agentic Coding."

    The [Google Antigravity prompts](/prompts/google-antigravity) in this collection were shared by the community and appear to be internal system prompts for an agentic pair-programming AI. They have not been officially confirmed or released by Google.
  </Accordion>
